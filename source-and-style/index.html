Welcome to the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC)

UIUC is a leading public research university known for its innovative research and vibrant campus life. Established in 1867, UIUC offers a wide range of programs and is committed to academic excellence and student success.

  • Strong emphasis on research and innovation
